Our Story

Tradition, Scooped Fresh

At Michoacana Premium Ice Cream & Paletería, we bring the spirit of classic Mexican neverías to every cone, cup, and paleta. From fruity aguas frescas to loaded mangonadas and creamy malteadas, everything is crafted to feel like a celebration.

We believe the best treats start simple: quality ingredients, bold flavors, and service that makes you smile. Whether you’re craving a classic nieve de cono or a spicy-sweet snack, there’s something here for everyone.

  • 🍦 Classic & creative ice cream flavors
  • 🍡 Fresh fruit & cream paletas
  • 🥭 Mangonadas, raspados & aguas
  • 🌶️ Preparados, tostilocos & snacks
