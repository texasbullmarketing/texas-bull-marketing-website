Spirit of 1908

Roots & gathering

Nostalgia with a dash of mystery & magic.

Seguin has always known how to gather - saloons, corner bars, downtown nights that turn strangers into regulars. The 1908 picks up that thread in a historic Court Street building with a modern dual-vibe twist.

Upstairs energy. Downstairs intrigue. Eclectic antiques, Texas art, and a room that still feels like a local secret - even when it's full.

"The 1908 isn't just another bar; it's a nod to the town's rich history of saloons and gathering spots with a modern twist." - Seguin Gazette
