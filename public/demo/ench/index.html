Since day one

A true local spot in Nixon.

La Enchiladita is the kind of place locals count on — warm, no-frills, and full of flavor. We make our tortillas fresh and serve up the classics the way folks around here like them: hearty cheese enchiladas, carne guisada, puffy tacos, and breakfast tacos that hit the spot.

Nothing fancy. Just good, honest Tex-Mex in a cozy dining room with friendly faces and plenty of coffee. Perfect for breakfast on the way to work, a quick lunch, or Friday evening when we open back up for dinner.
